Habit-Tracker-Vue

April 26, 2021 2-minute read
Xuan Jin
Uncategorized
Uncategorized

I, procrastinate a lot, like really a lot. I was thinking to have a habit tracking app to help myself to form a habit, BUT, I have been delaying on it. How ironic! Hahahah, I can hear your laugh through my screen.

Even though there are things like Habitica exist, but that app is too busy, I love the RPG type, but I don’t need the app to be like that. The productive one is simple, but I want some other stuff. I am envision it to be quick and simple. There will be a calendar, and you click on the date of the calendar, it will have a modal show up where it is asking you to 1) add activities you want to track; 2) check off the activities you already done with; 3) if you checked off all of the activities that you want to form a habit, it will show green, if none or less than half done, then it will be red, if partial done, meaning more than half done, it will show green (this part may be a stretch…I am honestly not sure how to do this part yet as of today) 4) there is a list view to see all of the activities you are tracking, next to each activities, you can see a count of how many days you have completed, it also doesn’t matter if you skipped a day in the middle, because we all know keep up is hard LOL. I also have the ambition of making this work on the phone and on the web, the web will be able to track and analyze data, but your phone is the data entering point. This is a big project, I don’t know if I will actually get to it, but I think I know how to get to the point to make half of it work. That’s a start, right?

So where do we start? I have no clue…I guess the first step is to make a calendar working with vue, that’s the first step. (day of 4/25/2021)
