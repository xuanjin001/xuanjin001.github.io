Our Snack is Peanuts!!!!!!

So we all have those times, when we really want to have something as snacks. Here I am going to introduce a quick snack where you can finish in 7 minutes.

HERE WE GO!

  • Material

    • 1 cup of raw shelled peanuts

    • Oil spray

    • Salt

  • Prep:

    • Preheat oven to 375F

  • Cooking steps: (time expected 7 minutes in total)

    • Put 1 cup of raw shelled peanuts on the bake pan, spray oil (I like to use pan oil spray a lot) on those, then spray salt on the peanuts (depends on if you like salty stuff or not)

    • When the oven is ready, put your peanuts in there for 2 minutes, then take out and try to rotate the peanuts for a little bit, then put it back to the oven for another 5 minutes.

    • After take them out, please cool them down for a little bit, then you can enjoy!!!

YOU ARE DONE!!!!
