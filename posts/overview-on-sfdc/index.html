Overview on SFDC

February 6, 2017 2-minute read
Xuan Jin
Developer
Developer

Standard objects from Salesforce:

Accounts

` `

  • Organizations that you need to track, such as customers

` `

  • Have related contacts, opportunities and cases

` `

  • Accounts to other objects: one to many relationships

Contacts

` `

  • Individuals associated with your accounts

` `

  • Have related cases

Opportunities

` `

  • Potential sales associated with your accounts

` `

  • Make up your pipeline and forecasts

` `

  • Associated with contacts

Cases

` `

  • Customer feedback, problems, or questions

` `

  • Associated with accounts and contacts

` `

  • Used to resolve issues

Solutions

` `

  • Descriptions of customer issues and resolutions

` `

  • Associated with cases

Campaigns

` `

  • Marketing projects

` `

  • Related leads and contacts shows as campaign members

` `

  • Have related opportunities

Leads

` `

  • Individuals interested in your products or services

` `

  • Associated to one or more account

` `

  • Converted into accounts, contacts, and opportunities

SFDC Objects and Relationships

` `

  • Account associated with contact, opportunities and case

Related lists on parent record, look ups on related records
