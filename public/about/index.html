My honest take on AI right now Link to heading

I wrote a longer post about this if you want the full version. Short version:

I use Claude Code for scaffolding and architecture. Gemini via AI Studio for quick experiments and prototyping. I’ve tested DeepSeek, Ollama, Cursor, a bunch of others. The tools keep changing, but what stays the same is — you still have to know what you’re building. You still have to stay engaged. It’s really easy to just let the model do everything and stop thinking. I try not to do that.

AI is a really good co-worker. It’s not a replacement for actually understanding things.
